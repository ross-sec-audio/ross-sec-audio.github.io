Uncertainty

Carnacopia

Ross Prendergast · Carnacopia

I wrote this short sketch using a bunch of delays and some resonators to get that slightly carnatic sound (However I appreciate this is not Carnatic music).
This track was recorded and produced using Ableton Live.

Entropy

Ross Prendergast · Entropy

I wrote this track back in 2014 while traveling in New Zealand. My wife to be introduced me to Liquid Drum & Bass and I was hooked. I listened to a lot of Commix and produced this soon after. It’s not perfect but I enjoyed producing it.
This track was produced using Ableton Live.

Moloko - Knee Deepen (Remix)

Ross Prendergast · Moloko - Knee Deepen Remix

RadioHead - Reckoner (Remix)
